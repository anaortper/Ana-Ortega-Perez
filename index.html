About

Teaching, research, and language education

Ana Ortega Pérez is a lecturer at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Her academic trajectory brings together Spanish linguistics, second language acquisition, communication, journalism, and international relations.

She holds a Ph.D. in Spanish from UC Davis and a Ph.D. in Communications from the Universidad de Sevilla. She also holds a Master’s degree in International Relations from the Universidad Internacional de Andalucía and a B.A. in Journalism from the Universidad de Sevilla.
