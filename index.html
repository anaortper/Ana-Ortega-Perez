Language shapes how we learn, belong, and participate.

My work examines how language, identity, and educational practices intersect to create more equitable learning environments. Through research, teaching, and collaborative projects, I explore heritage language education, critical language awareness, plain language, media studies, and intercultural communicative competence to bridge scholarship and classroom practice.
